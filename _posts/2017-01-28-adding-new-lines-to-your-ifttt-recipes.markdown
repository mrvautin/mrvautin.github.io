Adding breaks or new lines to your ifttt recipes can be a difficult task. Facebook seems to be particularly picky with it's new line characters where standard new lines like `\r` `\r\n` and `<br>` are ignored.

**The solution**:

`<br>&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;<br>`
